# store-analysis
my project "super sample store"as apart of m intern i started by collecting data and explore it then extract data to power query and clean it making transformation then load data and start to analyze it with showing weak area 
 1. Products Analysis
Insights:
*Total Quantity by Category: Technology for 60.48% of total quantity, indicating it is the most demanded category, while Furniture (18.32%) has the lowest demand.
*Total Profit by Sub-Category: Categories like "Binders" and "Paper" generate more profits, whereas sub-categories like "Tables" and "Bookcases" show lower profitability.
*Total Sales by Category: Each category contributes equally to sales (33.33%), suggesting a balanced distribution of revenue.
-Weak Area:
Furniture's quantity is significantly lower (7k), even though its sales are equal to the other categories, indicating inefficiency in volume sales.
Sub-categories like "Tables" and "Bookcases" require strategies to improve profitability.
2-Customers Analysis
Insights:
-Sales by Segment: The "Consumer" segment contributes the most to sales (1.16M), while the "Home Office" segment contributes the least (0.43M).
-Sub-category by Segment: "Binders" and "Paper" dominate in terms of sub-category sales across all segments. Sub-categories like "Copiers" and "Machines" have significantly lower sales.
-Weak Area:
-The "Home Office" segment is underperforming compared to other segments. Marketing and sales strategies need to be revisited for this segment.
-Sub-categories like "Copiers" and "Machines" have minimal sales; reasons should be identified (e.g., pricing, availability, or demand)
3-Location Analysis
Insights:
-Sum of Quantity by Region: The "South" region accounts for 29.04% of quantities sold, the highest among regions. The "West" region (16.38%) has the lowest quantity share.
-Sum of Sales by Region: Sales are highest in the "Central" region (31.58%) and lowest in the "West" region (17.0%).
-Sum of Sales by Ship Mode: Standard Class shipping is the most popular and effective, whereas "Same Day" contributes the least to sales.
*Weak Area:
The "West" region has both the lowest sales and quantities sold. Marketing efforts should focus here.
"Same Day" shipping has low sales and might need cost optimization or targeted promotions.
4-Recommendations
Furniture Improvement:
Introduce promotional campaigns or bundle offers to increase sales volume.
Optimize pricing strategies for low-profit sub-categories like "Tables" and "Bookcases."
Regional Focus:
Allocate resources to boost sales in the "West" region with targeted advertising.
Evaluate supply chain effectiveness and product availability in underperforming regions.
Shipping Optimization:
Assess the feasibility of reducing "Same Day" shipping costs.
Encourage customers to opt for faster delivery through incentives
